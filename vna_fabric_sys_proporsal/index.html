Solving Current Challenges

Current Fragmented Process

1. No Remote Access: Decisions made while traveling are based on old information.

2. Inefficient GRN Process: Manual Excel uploads are slow and lead to data errors.

3. Disconnected Warehouse: Admins use a PC, but warehouse staff have no digital tool, causing delays.

4. Difficult Data Management: Complex, desktop-only UI makes managing stock difficult.

Proposed Unified Platform

1. Global Access: Real-time data available on any device, anywhere.

2. Digital GRN: Direct data input from supplier packing lists, with auto-validation.

3. Connected Warehouse: A tablet app for staff syncs instantly with the admin's view.

4. Simple Management: A clean, web-based interface for all data management tasks.

Module: Invoicing & Financials

Financial Clarity

Instantly see who owes money and manage payments effectively. The system automates complex tasks to ensure accuracy.

  • Automated Invoicing: Invoices are generated directly from finalized Dispatch Notes, reducing manual work.
  • Handles Complex Billing: Automatically creates separate invoices for items from different shipments, as per your business rules.
  • Partial Payment Tracking: Easily record and track multiple payments against a single invoice.
  • Outstanding Reports: Generate real-time outstanding payment statements for any customer at any time.
